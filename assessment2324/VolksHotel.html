Volks Hotel – The Creative Home for Nomads in Amsterdam

Dive into a dynamic environment where creativity meets community at Volks Hotel. Located in Amsterdam's East district, this trendy hotspot offers an inspiring workspace combined with a vibrant social scene. Whether you're brainstorming your next big idea or networking with fellow nomads, Volks Hotel provides the perfect backdrop for productive work & meaningful connections.

Great Ambiance

Volks Hotel combines modern design with a lively atmosphere, perfect for creative work sessions.

Fast WiFi

Stay connected with high-speed internet access throughout the entire hotel workspace.

Great Coffee

Enjoy premium coffee and a full menu to keep you energized throughout your workday.

What is Like to Work at Volks Hotel

At Volks Hotel, you'll find yourself in a buzzing hub of creativity and innovation. The open layout encourages collaboration while still offering quiet corners for focused work. With its industrial-chic design, comfortable seating, and abundant natural light, the space inspires productivity. The hotel's community-driven approach means you'll always find like-minded professionals to connect with over coffee or at one of their regular networking events.

Why You Should Visit Volks Hotel Too

Experience the digital nomad lifestyle at its finest. Volks Hotel is more than just a workspace – it's a community hub where innovation thrives. From early morning coffee sessions to late-night brainstorming, this venue offers the flexibility and amenities that modern remote workers need. Plus, with excellent connectivity to the rest of Amsterdam, you're never far from the city's vibrant culture and attractions.
